    Welcome to our World

    In world entrenched in both the technological might of the post industrial revolution and steeped in the ancient lore of magic, where blades cross and the roar of gunpowder is heard, an expedition to a once closed-off land awaits. Industrial powers fight each other with sheer weight of cash and market shares, while soldiers in the field turn machine guns and arcane-mechanical automatons onto dangers like trolls and rebels. Each nation strives to make a larger mark on the world than its' adversaries, and any single person has to strive to stand out in the face of such powers. In the frontier town of Aldgate, a revolver, a keen eye, and a good deal of grit can get you by as you try to uncover the mysteries of a hidden past, navigate the tensions between old empires and rising powers, and put it all on the line for the next payday. Welcome to Sins of a Nation.

    Sins of a Nation: The Sixth Kingdom is a Full Metal Fantasy LARP, with technology and magic sharing focus in the setting. In the past, the world of Ora was a classic fantasy setting, but has advanced past the industrial revolution, and now factories dot the landscape, alongside old mages towers and eldritch forests. The classic fantasy elements are present in new ways, and the setting looks to let players explore these familiar territories in ways that are less familiar.
